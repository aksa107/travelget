Popular Places

Munnar

A hill station located in the Western Ghats mountain range, known for its tea plantations and beautiful natural scenery.

Alleppey

A city known for its beautiful backwaters, where travelers can take boat rides and explore the waterways.

Kochi (Cochin)

A city known for its mix of modern and traditional architecture, as well as its historical sites such as the Dutch Palace and the Jewish Synagogue

Kovalam Beach

It is known for its stunning beaches and Ayurvedic massage centers, making it a popular destination for travelers looking to relax and rejuvenate.

Wayanad

A district in the Western Ghats known for its rolling hills, lush forests, and picturesque valleys.

Athirapilly Waterfalls

Located in the Thrissur district, these waterfalls are known for their stunning views and serene surroundings.

Varkala

A beach town known for its beautiful beaches, as well as its ancient Janardanaswamy Temple.

Thekkady

A region in the Western Ghats known for its Periyar National Park, home to a variety of animals including elephants, tigers, and deer.
